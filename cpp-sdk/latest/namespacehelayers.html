◆ CrfNanHandlingMethod

enum helayers::CrfNanHandlingMethod

An enum specifying how "nan" predicted probabilites are handled in CrfPlain class.

"nan" preicted probabilities are the result of predicting samples that do not fullfill the condition of any leaf in the trees of the completely random forest. This may happen, for example, for samples that some of their predictors contain "0" in all possible categories. This enum specifies the algorithm enacted when predicting such samples.

Enumerator
REPLACE_WITH_USER_VALUE 

In "REPLACE_WITH_USER_VALUE" nan handling method, the predicted probabilities are set to fixed values decided by the user.

REPLACE_WITH_DATA_RATIO 

In "REPLACE_WITH_DATA_RATIO" nan handling method, the predicted probability of a category c is set to the weighted ratio of training samples which had the categroy c.

(for category weights see the setCategoryWeights() description).

RETURN_NAN 

In "RETURN_NAN" nan handling method, "nan" probabilities are returned.
